## How do I update to the latest version?
Duplicate your theme, then download and apply the new version. See [Upgrade guide](/themes/release/upgrade-guides/v2-2-to-v2-3).

## My swatches aren't showing?
Ensure the variant option is named **Color** and Variant selector = **Swatches**.
